Hummus

The Best Hummus Ever

You will never need to buy hummus at the store again. It only takes a few minutes, but the taste is out of this world.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of chickpeas, drained
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Ice water as needed

Steps

  1. In a food processor, add chickpeas, cumin, taini, salt, lemon zest, lemon juice.
  2. Start blending in food processor, scraping down sides with rubber spatula
  3. Add ice water in 1 tbsp increments as needed until desired consistency is reached.
