Spaghetti

This spaghetti is delicious.

This spaghetti will knock your socks off. What sets this dish apart from the others is the use of herbs and spices. This is not your standard weeknight meal. It's a little more work than dumping a jar of spaghetti sauce over some noodles, but it is worth it!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsps olive oil
  • Noodles, spaghetti, angel hair, or some other long pasta
  • Marinara sauce, 12 oz
  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste, from a tube
  • 1 cup reserved pasta water

Steps

  1. Boil pot of salted water for pasta.
  2. In a separate saucepan, turn heat on medium and put the oil in
  3. Once oil is shimmering, add diced onions along with a pinch of salt until golden brown, stirring regularly
  4. Add garlic along with dried herbs and stir frequently for 1 minunte
  5. Add tomato paste and stir frequently until it begins to darken in color, about 1 minute
  6. Deglaze the plan with water as needed
  7. Add 1/4 cup reserved pasta water and stir, until at desired consistency
  8. Add more pasta water if needed
  9. Add noodles to the sauce and stir with tongs
