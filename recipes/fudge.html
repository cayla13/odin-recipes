Fudge

This fudge is so good.

This fudge is so easy to make, 100 decadent, and 100% delicious.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz dark chocolate, at least 65%
  • 4 oz toasted hazlenuts, chopped
  • 1 8 oz can sweetended condensed coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar

Steps

  1. To a double boiler, add chocolate and stir continuously
  2. Once chocolate is melted, slowly pour coconut milk in
  3. Fold in hazelnuts and pour into a parchment paper lined pan
  4. Let cool on the counter for 1 hour, and then in the refridgerator for at least 2 hours.
  5. Slice into 1 inch pieces and enjoy!
